HOW TO BUILD
Windows:
call "C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Visual Studio 14.0\VC\vcvarsall.bat" [x64/x86]
msbuild.exe voidstorm.sln /p:Configuration=[Debug/Release]

Linux:
make config=[debug[32/64]/release[32/64]]

Luajit 5.1
SDL2
